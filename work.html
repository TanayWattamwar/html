• Economic Reforms of 1991

    • -As Finance Minister, Singh spearheaded transformative reforms that liberalized India’s economy. His efforts ended decades of protectionism, dismantled the License Raj, and opened India to global markets, setting the stage for rapid economic growth.

  • India-US Civil Nuclear Agreement (2008)

    • -As Prime Minister, Singh negotiated this historic deal to provide India with access to nuclear technology for civilian use. This agreement ended decades of nuclear isolation and elevated India’s position on the global stage.

  • Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)

    • -Singh’s government introduced MGNREGA, which provided legal guarantees of employment to rural households, aiming to reduce poverty and unemployment in rural areas.
