ACHIEVEMENTS

  1. Architect of India’s Economic Reforms (1991)

    2. -As Finance Minister, Manmohan Singh was pivotal in liberalizing India's economy. He introduced policies that dismantled the License Raj, opened the economy to foreign investments, and laid the foundation for India’s rapid economic growth. This included devaluation of the rupee, reduction in import tariffs, and industrial deregulation.

  2. Prime Minister for Two Terms (2004-2014)

    3. -Singh was India’s first Sikh Prime Minister and led the country for a decade. His tenure saw significant economic growth, technological advancement, and social reforms. He maintained a steady and stable economic trajectory despite global challenges like the 2008 financial crisis.

  3. Signing of the India-US Civil Nuclear Agreement (2008)

    4. -This landmark deal ended India’s nuclear isolation and enabled the country to access nuclear technology and fuel for civilian energy purposes. It bolstered India’s international standing and strengthened relations with the United States.
